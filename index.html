The Event Flow

A meticulously planned schedule to ensure a smooth journey from start to finish.

08:00 AM

Arrival and Registration

Teams check-in at the main desk, receive their kits, and finalize their registration details.

09:00 AM

Opening Ceremony

Official welcome address by the founders and introduction to the core themes of Ignite'26.

09:45 AM

Event Distribution

Teams receive final briefings and are escorted to their respective competition zones.

10:00 AM

Round 1 Begins

Competitions officially commence simultaneously across all four event categories.

01:00 PM

A networking lunch break for participants to recharge before the final rounds.

05:00 PM

Awards presentation, closing remarks, and celebration of the day's achievements.
